About

Es FÜGT sich explores adaptable joints for structural concrete reuse. We design connections that let reclaimed slabs and walls meet again—precisely, reversibly, and with tolerance for demolition irregularities.

The work narrows the gap between research and practice through prototyped joints, on-site measurements and a compact catalogue for design teams.
